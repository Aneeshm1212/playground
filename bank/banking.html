Internet Banking Registration

Axis Bank ensures hassle free internet banking registrations in just a few easy steps. Online banking is secure with the added two-step authentication system of Netsecure. Follow the below steps for Internet banking registration and start banking online. Quick, easy steps to login and start banking online with ease

Follow these steps if you do not have username and password:

  • Get Registered to your account through registration form
  • Fill your account details
  • Read the instruction before signing
  • The form must be signed by account holders
  • While submitting the form at the branch, register your mobile number for Internet Banking (Netsecure), in case it's not registered. (This 2nd factor authentication ensures that you enjoy an additional security layer for all your important online transactions like fund transfer, bill payment, mobile recharge etc.)
  • You can also register your mobile number by visiting your bank or through nearest Swipe Bank ATM.
  • Bank will send your password through email
  • After filling in required details, your registration has been completed
  • Start transacting and enjoy all services of Swipe Internet Banking

Follow these steps to register your mobile number for internet banking at ATM:

  • Visit your nearest Swipe Bank ATM
  • Select Registration
  • Select Netsecure
  • Enter your Account details and Mobile number
  • You will receive a sms regarding registration
  • Alternative option is to visit your nearest bank branch for mobile linking

Get your customer ID

  • Your Customer ID is mentioned on the welcome letter and cheque book that is mailed to you post your account opening.
  • You can also SMS CUSTID to 56161600 from your registered mobile number to get your Customer ID*.
  • Example: CUSTID 913XXXXXXXXXXXX
    * SMS charges as specified by your operator will apply for the SMS requests sent by you.

For Retail/Agri-Rural Customers

  • To login to internet banking, you need the login id which is the same as your 9-digit Customer ID (mentioned on the welcome letter and printed on your cheque book), along with your login password.
  • You can also obtain your Customer ID by sending an SMS CUSTID to 56161600 .
  • New users can generate a password online by using the ‘First time user option’. You can generate the login password using your debit card 4-digit ATM Pin and your registered mobile number.
  • If you have not applied for a Debit Card, a printed password will be delivered to you by courier.
  • To login for first time, click here
  • In case you have forgotten your password click on "Forgot Password" link on the login page and generate your password online.
